Creamy Honey

Our Creamy Honey comes from 100% pure organic honey that has been processed to control crystallization. Due to this formation of tiny crystals it gives the honey a creamy and smooth texture, perfect for a spread or used as an alternative to sugar in drinks. Even though it is called creamed, our creamy honey contains no dairy products.
