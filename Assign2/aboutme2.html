Demographic

Kuching is the capital and the most populous city in Malaysia's largest state, Sarawak. The name Kuching was derived from the Malay word for "cat". Kuching has an average population of 325,132 consists of Malays, Chinese, Iban, Bidayuh and others. Kuching is known for its local foods such as laksa, kolo mee, Sarawak Layer Cake "Kek lapis" and more. Kuching also have a lot of attractive locations to visit.

Sabah is a state of Malaysia above Sarawak. Its is nicknamed "Land below the wind". Kota Kinabalu is the capital of Sabah. Sabah is famously known for The Mount Kinabalu where many travelers ought to climb or just visit. Sabah also have many islands and natural tropical rainforests for people who desires nature.
