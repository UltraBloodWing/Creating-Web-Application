Honeycomb

Do you know how important honey bees are for our survival? Very important. In fact, there are numerous studies made in this area which concludes that the survival of Humans depends on bees. They are to be thanked for the pollination of 80% of the worldâ€™s plants including 90 different food crops.

Therefore, we decided to honor our little friends who are truly important for our survival, by not making their hard work go to waste. In the extraction of honey from the honeycombs a lot of them get damaged. Hence, it cannot be put back in the tray. But rather we figured out a brilliant way for the honeycomb to be used and for us to be thankful for the bees.

With a lot of work done on R&D, we came up with a wide variety of products made by fresh Honeycombs to the liking of various taste. Some of the favorites include chocolates, Magnums and Dried Honeycomb snacks. Also, we provide fresh honeycombs in honey jars.

Our company gives a lot of importance for quality control, and further research and development for honey farming in a sustainable way. All of our natural resources are harvested in our in-house farms located in Sarawak, Malaysia.
