My Hometown

My hometown Kuching is the capital and the most populous city in the state of Sarawak in Malaysia. Out city's 325,000 residents consist of an almost equal number of Malay and Chinese Malaysians as well as sizeable Dayal communities. Many travel brochures refer to Kuching as 'Cat City'. Kuching is also a major food destination for tourists and the main gateaway for travellers visiting Sarawak. Local delicacies of Kuching including bamboo chicken, kolo mee, laksa, and the most famous is Sarawak Layer Cake. Other than food, Kuching also have many beautiful beach, national park, rainforest, and orangutan rehabilitation centre.
